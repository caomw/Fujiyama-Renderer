Fujiyama Renderer v0.1.2(Alpha) Release Notes
=============================================

New features since v0.1.1
-------------------------
 * Subsurface scattering shader has been implemented. The key properties are
   scattering_coefficient and absorption_coefficient. More intuitive properties
   for artists will be added in the future.

 * obj2mesh has been added. Now you can render lots of assets of obj formats.

 * Fixed a lots of minor bugs and updated documentation.

Features under development
--------------------------
 * Deep shadow
 * Displacement shader
 * Subdivision surface
 * Alembic support
